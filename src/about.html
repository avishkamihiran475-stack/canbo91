About Hotel Canbo

Hotel Canbo is a welcoming hotel resort located in the peaceful town of Bibile, Uva Province, Sri Lanka. Designed for comfort, relaxation, and friendly hospitality, our hotel offers guests a pleasant stay with easy access to local attractions, dining options, and travel routes. Whether you are visiting for a short stay, family trip, business visit, or relaxing getaway, Hotel Canbo is ready to welcome you.

Contact Us
OUR STORY

Located on Mahiyangana Road, Bibile

Hotel Canbo is strategically located on Mahiyangana Road in Bibile, offering easy access to major travel routes while maintaining a peaceful, secluded atmosphere. Our property combines the convenience of a roadside location with the tranquility of Sri Lanka's countryside. We've created a space where guests can enjoy modern comfort while experiencing the natural beauty and warm hospitality that Uva Province is known for.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide every guest with a comfortable, clean, and relaxing stay supported by friendly service, quality dining, and convenient booking options.
