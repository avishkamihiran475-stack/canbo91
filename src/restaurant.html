Restaurant

Enjoy delicious meals in a relaxing and elegant dining atmosphere.

At Hotel Canbo, we believe that great food is an essential part of a great stay. Our restaurant serves fresh, flavorful meals prepared with locally sourced ingredients. From traditional Sri Lankan rice and curry to international favorites, every dish is crafted with care and served in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
